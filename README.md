Zomato Analytics Dashboard | Power BI
------------------------------------------------
**Project Overview**
The Zomato Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze food delivery performance, restaurant reviews, and customer preferences.
This dashboard helps stakeholders identify trends, track key metrics, and make data-driven decisions to improve operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

**Objectives**
Track order and delivery performance
Monitor restaurant ratings and customer reviews
Understand customer behavior and cuisine preferences
Enable quick decision-making using KPIs
Improve operational efficiency through data insights

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**
Total Orders – Number of orders placed in the selected period
Average Delivery Time – Mean delivery time (in minutes)
Average Order Value (AOV) – Revenue per order
Customer Satisfaction Rating – Average customer rating (1–5)
Discount Utilization Rate – Percentage of orders with discounts

**Dashboard Visualizations**
Total Orders Trend – Line chart showing order volume over time
Delivery Time Analysis – Average delivery time by city
Top 10 Restaurants – Restaurants ranked by total revenue
Customer Ratings Distribution – Breakdown of ratings from 1 to 5
Cuisine Popularity – Most popular cuisines based on order count

**Filters & Slicers**
Date Range
City / Location
Cuisine Type
Rating Range
Discount Applied

**Data Sources**
Orders Dataset
Order ID, Customer Location, Restaurant Name, Cuisine, Order Date
Delivery Time, Order Amount, Discount Applied
Restaurant Dataset
Restaurant Name, Location, Cuisine, Ratings
Reviews Dataset
Customer Ratings, Review Date, Feedback

**Tools & Technologies**
Power BI – Dashboard development & visualization
Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
DAX – KPI calculations and measures

**Functional Highlights**
Dynamic KPIs based on selected filters
City-wise delivery performance comparison
Revenue-based restaurant ranking
Cuisine demand analysis
Customer satisfaction tracking

**Business Value**
Helps operations teams monitor delivery efficiency
Enables marketing teams to understand customer preferences
Assists restaurant owners in tracking performance and ratings
Supports data-driven strategic decision-making
